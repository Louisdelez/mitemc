Without a wooden pickaxe, the path to flint tools requires foraging.

## Sticks

- **Leaves drop sticks.** Each leaf block has a base 4% chance to drop a stick when broken; decaying leaves trigger the same roll at 25% of that rate.
- Sticks naturally spawn under oak/birch trees in newly generated chunks (Phase 2).

## Flint shards

Flint shards are obtained from gravel like vanilla flint, but at higher yield. Knap two shards together (or with a stick) to obtain crafting components.

## Plants & food

- **Apples** drop from oak leaves at vanilla rate.
- **Berries** can be picked from sweet berry bushes (vanilla).
- Phase 5 adds onions and the crop blight system; for now, vanilla farming applies.
