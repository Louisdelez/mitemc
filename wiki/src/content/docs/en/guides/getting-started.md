## Install

1. Install **NeoForge 26.1.x** (matches MC 26.1).
2. Drop `mitemc-26.1.x-0.1.0.jar` into your `mods/` folder.
3. Launch. The mod logs `MITEMC bootstrap — Minecraft Is Too Easy, again.` if it loaded correctly.

:::note
MITEMC requires Java 21. Modern Minecraft launchers ship the right runtime automatically.
:::

## Your first ten minutes

1. **You spawn with 3 hearts and 3 food icons.** Don't take risks. Falling 4 blocks kills you.
2. **You can't punch wood for a pickaxe.** Look for **sticks under trees** or punch leaves: there's a chance each leaf drops one.
3. With 1 stick + 1 flint shard you can knap a **flint hatchet** in the crafting grid. The hatchet is your gate to logs.
4. Eat anything you can — apples from leaves, berries, raw meat. Hunger drains even when you stand still (basal metabolism).
5. Stay out of rain. It accelerates hunger.
6. When food drops below 1 icon you become **too weak to break or place blocks**, so always keep a snack.
