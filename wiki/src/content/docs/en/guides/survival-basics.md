## The Stone Age start

You begin as a hunter-gatherer. Until you have flint tools, you cannot harvest stone, ores, or hard wood. Your priorities, in order:

1. **Sticks.** Punch leaves or pick them up under trees.
2. **Flint.** Found in gravel like vanilla — but you only need shards, not full flint.
3. **Hatchet.** 2 sticks + 2 flint shards = flint hatchet. Now you can chop logs.
4. **Shelter.** A 1-block alcove sealed with dirt is enough for night one.
5. **Food.** Apples from oak leaves, berries, fish if you reach water.

## Survival rules to internalize

- **Hunger ticks while idle.** Don't stand still for long.
- **Rain accelerates hunger.** Cover up when it rains.
- **Healing is slow.** Don't take fights you can avoid.
- **Mobs see further and chase longer.** Sneaking past is harder.
- **Creative is disabled.** This is intentional.

## What kills you on day one

| Cause            | How to avoid                                        |
|------------------|-----------------------------------------------------|
| 4-block fall     | Stay on flat ground; build dirt steps.              |
| Single zombie    | 3-heart pool — one hit takes a third of your life.  |
| Starvation       | Always carry one piece of food.                     |
| Drowning         | Don't enter water without a flint knife to cut weed.|
| Cold rain        | Roof over your head before nightfall.               |
