## What is MITEMC?

**MITEMC** is a port of [Avernite's MITE](https://www.minecraftforum.net/forums/mapping-and-modding-java-edition/minecraft-mods/1294284-minecraft-is-too-easy-mite-mod) (Minecraft Is Too Easy) to **Minecraft 26.1** on **NeoForge**. The original ran only on MC 1.6.4 and stopped updating in 2017; this project rebuilds it on modern foundations while staying faithful to the design.

## Project status

All **8 phases** of the 1:1 port have shipped: 50 tools, 4 ores, 4 furnaces, 10 hostile mobs, an agriculture system with blight, 7 enchantments, a player-locked strongbox, 5 lore books, 35 advancements, full configuration. See the closed [roadmap](https://github.com/MITEMC/mitemc/blob/main/ROADMAP.md), the [changelog](https://github.com/MITEMC/mitemc/blob/main/CHANGELOG.md), or the [extensions plan](/en/reference/extensions/) for what comes next.
